B — Editorial, no icons on landing

Numbered headlines instead of icon pills. Reads like a magazine masthead — and Instrument Serif gets a stage. Removes Sparkles AND removes the bigger "SaaS landing template" feel in one move. Admin/waitlist still get clean icon swaps.

My pick: Option B. The "icon + label x4" pattern is itself part of what reads as AI-generated — swapping just one icon (A) treats the symptom; B treats the cause and shows off the new type direction. If you'd rather keep things small, A is safe.
