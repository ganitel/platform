## ✅ Point 1 — Project Presentation

### 📌 Project Name

**Ganitel** — Strategic rebranding of the former **Résidence MG** housing platform.

---

### 🎯 Main Objective

Ganitel aims to become the **leading platform in Cameroon** for booking vacation accommodations.
This launch represents the **first phase** of a larger vision to **digitally transform African tourism**, offering travelers an **emotionally fulfilling and culturally rooted homecoming experience**.

> **Mission**: To provide every traveler with the comfort of home, wherever they choose to explore in Africa.
> **Tagline**: *Home is a warm experience. Book it, Feel it.*

---

### 🧩 User Problem

Currently, booking quality accommodations in Africa—especially in Cameroon—is often:

* **Fragmented**, with services scattered across various informal channels,
* **Unreliable**, with little to no verified information,
* **Manual**, often requiring phone calls or WhatsApp coordination.

Ganitel addresses these pain points by offering a **centralized, seamless, and emotionally engaging booking experience**.

---

### 👥 Target Audience

* 🎯 **Primary target at launch**:
  Members of the **Cameroonian diaspora** returning home for vacations.

* 🧍‍♂️ **User persona prioritized in MVP**:
  The **Single-Service Booker**, i.e. a user who simply wants to **book a place to stay**—without building a full trip or customizing packages.

---

### 📈 Key Success Indicators (KPIs)

* 💳 **Primary KPI**:
  Number of users who **complete a successful transaction** (i.e., reach checkout and pay for accommodation).

* 📣 **Strategic milestone**:
  Achieve **strong user adoption within Cameroon** during Year 1.

* 📊 **Secondary indicators** (to be refined):

  * Conversion rate (visitor → booking)
  * Average session duration
  * Post-booking satisfaction rating
  * Number of active listings onboarded

---

### 🛠️ MVP Scope

The **Minimum Viable Product (MVP)** will focus exclusively on **accommodation booking**, with:

* A streamlined **single-service booking flow**
* A data structure and backend designed for **future expansion** to:

  * Car rentals
  * Restaurants & dining
  * Curated activity packages

---

### 🌍 Long-Term Vision

* Build a **pan-African digital tourism ecosystem**
* Extend the platform to include complementary services
* Position Ganitel as **a cultural brand**, not just a technical tool
